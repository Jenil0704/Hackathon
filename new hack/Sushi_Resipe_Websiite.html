Sushi Recipe

Sushi is a Japanese delicacy that combines vinegared rice with various ingredients, such as raw fish, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits. It's enjoyed worldwide for its fresh flavors and artistry.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 40 minutes
  • Preparation: 30 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of sushi rice
  • 2 Â½ cups of water
  • 3 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Fresh raw fish (e.g., tuna, salmon)
  • Vegetables (e.g., cucumber, avocado)
  • Soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger for serving

Instructions

1. Cook the Rice: Rinse the rice thoroughly, cook it in water, and let it cool before adding the vinegar mixture (rice vinegar, sugar, and salt).

2. Prepare Fillings:Slice fish and vegetables into thin strips.

3. Assemble Sushi: Lay a nori sheet on a bamboo mat, spread rice, and add fillings.

4. Roll & Serve: Roll tightly using the mat, slice, and serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and ginger.
