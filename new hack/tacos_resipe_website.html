Tacos Recipe

Tacos, a staple of Mexican cuisine, consist of a corn or wheat tortilla folded around a variety of fillings such as meat, beans, and vegetables. They’re endlessly customizable and easy to make.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 15 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (corn or flour)
  • 300g ground beef or chicken
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Taco seasoning
  • Toppings: lettuce, cheese, sour cream, salsa, guacamole

Instructions

1. Cook Meat: Sauté onions and brown the meat with taco seasoning.

2. Warm Tortillas: Heat tortillas in a dry skillet.

3. Assemble Tacos: Place meat in the tortilla, and add your choice of toppings.
