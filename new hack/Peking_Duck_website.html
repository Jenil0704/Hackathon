Peking Duck Recipe

Peking Duck is a famous Chinese dish known for its crispy skin and succulent meat. Itâ€™s traditionally served with thin pancakes, hoisin sauce, and green onions.

Preparation Time

  • Total: 2 hours
  • Preparation: 30 minutes
  • Cooking: 1 hour 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 whole duck
  • Chinese five-spice, honey, soy sauce
  • Pancakes, hoisin sauce, cucumber, green onion

Instructions

1. Prep Duck: Marinate the duck with spices and air dry.

2. Roast Duck: Cook the duck until the skin is crispy.

3. Serve: Slice thinly and serve with pancakes and toppings.

4. Roll & Serve: Roll tightly using the mat, slice, and serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and ginger.
