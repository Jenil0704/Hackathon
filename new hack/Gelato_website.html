Gelato Recipe

Gelato is Italy’s take on ice cream, but it’s creamier, denser, and packed with flavor. It's typically made with milk, cream, sugar, and flavorings such as fruits, chocolate, or nuts.

Preparation Time

  • Total: 4 hours (includes freezing)
  • Preparation: 20 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup cream
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • Flavoring: vanilla, cocoa, or fruit purees

Instructions

1. Heat Base: Warm milk, cream, and sugar until dissolved.

2. Add Flavor: Stir in vanilla or other flavorings.

3. Churn: Freeze mixture in an ice cream machine until smooth.
