Pad Thai Recipe

Pad Thai is a classic Thai stir-fried noodle dish that blends sweet, sour, and salty flavors with a hint of spice. It's a popular street food and a staple in Thai restaurants.

Preparation Time

  • Total: 25 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 150g shrimp or tofu
  • 2 eggs, 1 onion, 2 tbsp tamarind paste, fish sauce, peanuts

Instructions

1. Soak Noodles: Soak rice noodles in warm water until soft, then drain.

2. Stir Fry: Cook shrimp or tofu, and scramble eggs with onions.

3. Add Noodles and Sauce: Toss noodles with tamarind paste, fish sauce, and stir-fry until combined. Top with peanuts.
