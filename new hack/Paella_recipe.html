Paella Recipe

Paella is a famous Spanish dish that blends rice with various ingredients like seafood, chicken, and vegetables. Saffron gives the dish its iconic flavor and color, creating a perfect meal for gatherings.

Preparation Time

  • Total: 1 hour
  • Preparation: 20 minutes
  • Cooking: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of paella rice
  • 2 ½ cups of water
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • ½ lb shrimp, mussels, and squid
  • Chicken thighs, chorizo
  • Saffron, garlic, paprika

Instructions

1. Sauté Meats and Vegetables: Brown chicken, cook chorizo, and add vegetables.

2. Add Rice and Broth: Stir in the rice, saffron, and broth.

3. Add Seafood: Place seafood on top and cook until rice is tender.
