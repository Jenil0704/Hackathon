Paella Recipe

Croissants are a French pastry known for their buttery, flaky texture. They require a bit of patience, but the result is a light, airy treat perfect for breakfast or snacks.

Preparation Time

  • Total:12 hours (includes proofing time)
  • Preparation: 1 hour
  • Cooking: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 500g flour
  • 300g butter
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Yeast, milk, salt

Instructions

1. Prepare Dough:Make a yeast-based dough and allow it to rise.

2. Laminate Dough:Layer butter and fold the dough several times.

3. Bake:Shape into croissants, proof, and bake until golden brown.
