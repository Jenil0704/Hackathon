Biryani Recipe

Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful South Asian rice dish made with spices, rice, and either meat, fish, or vegetables. Itâ€™s known for its richness and unique aroma.

Preparation Time

  • Total Time: 1 hour
  • Preparation: 20 minutes
  • Cooking: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 500g basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or lamb
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • Biryani masala, yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, saffron, ghee

Instructions

1. Cook Rice: Parboil the rice with spices like bay leaves and cardamom.

2. Prepare Meat: Cook the meat with masala and yogurt until tender.

3. Layering: Layer the rice and meat, drizzle with saffron-infused milk, and cook on low heat.
