About Us

Discover India with Flavours

At Indian Culinary Tourism, we are passionate about promoting culinary tourism in India and showcasing the country's rich gastronomic heritage to the world. Our vision is to make India the world's favorite culinary destination, where travelers can experience the diverse flavors and traditions that make Indian cuisine truly unique.

Founded out of our deep love for food and appreciation for the incredible diversity of Indian culinary traditions, Indian Culinary Tourism aims to connect travelers with authentic food experiences across the country. Whether it's exploring the bustling markets of Delhi, sampling street food in Mumbai, or indulging in traditional thalis in Rajasthan, we believe that food has the power to create unforgettable memories and forge meaningful connections with local communities.

Through our website, we strive to provide travelers with a wealth of information on authentic foods from every region of India, enabling them to plan immersive culinary tours tailored to their preferences. By supporting local communities, promoting sustainable food practices, and celebrating India's culinary heritage, we hope to inspire a new generation of food explorers and cultural ambassadors.

Join us on a culinary journey like no other and discover the flavors of India, one bite at a time.
