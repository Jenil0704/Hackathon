Cooking with Seasonal Ingredients

Using seasonal ingredients ensures freshness and flavor. Here's a guide to ingredients by season:

  • Spring: Asparagus, peas, radishes.
  • Summer: Tomatoes, zucchini, peaches.
  • Autumn: Pumpkins, apples, squash.
  • Winter: Citrus fruits, root vegetables, kale.
